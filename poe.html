Path of Exile is a Free-To-Play Diablo-like loot based Action RPG. The developers, Grinding Gear Games is based out of New Zealand. They've been working on the game for just over 10 years now and they've made something truly special. It is a shining gem of a game with an amazing F2P model which is amoung the best of Free-To-Play games.
